Big Red Eats

Big Red Eats is my clone of Cornell Appdev's Eatery App. Written in Dart and utilizing the Flutter framework with some of its awesome third-party packages, BRE shows you the eateries around the Cornell campus and their opening times, menus, and more.

KTM Train Time (under development)

KTM Train Time is an app that shows passengers of Malaysia's KTM trains everything related to their commute including train times (duh!), connecting train lines and buses at KTM stations, and more. Also written in Dart with Flutter as well as Python with Flask for some data scraping tasks.
